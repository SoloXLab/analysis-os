# Domain pack: Business / Market

## Key objects
Market segment, competitor, channel, pricing tier, customer acquisition cost (CAC), lifetime value (LTV).

## Key metrics
Revenue growth rate, market share, CAC/LTV ratio, churn rate, gross margin.

## Common failure modes / root causes
- Revenue/growth change misattributed to a strategic decision when it's actually a market-wide macro shift (check competitor trends before concluding company-specific causality)
- Channel mix shift changing blended CAC without any actual efficiency change per channel
- Survivorship bias in customer analysis (only studying retained customers, not churned ones)
- Pricing change effects confounded with simultaneous product changes

## Diagnostic approach
Always establish a market-wide/competitor baseline before attributing a business metric change to an internal decision — see `market-discovery` and `competitive-research` skills for the evidence-gathering workflow this domain pack assumes.
